Marks on a long climb.

Recognition is never the goal — protecting the Himalaya is. But the awards and mandates entrusted to FOSEP affirm that the work holds, year after year, on ground that does not forgive shortcuts.

Recognition

Awards & designations.

A record built over two decades of conservation, health, and community work across the Darjeeling Hills and Siliguri.

2004

Indira Priyadarshini Vriksha Mitra (IPVM) Award

Conferred by the Government of India in recognition of pioneering afforestation and environmental stewardship — among the country's highest honours for tree conservation.

2011

Anirudh Bhargawa “Environment 2011 Award”

Awarded by INTACH, New Delhi, acknowledging sustained contributions to environmental protection in one of the Himalaya's most fragile landscapes.

Designation

Regional Resource Agency (RRA)

Entrusted by the Ministry of Environment & Forests as the Regional Resource Agency for National Environment Awareness Campaign (NEAC) activities across the Darjeeling Hills and Siliguri — a standing mandate to coordinate environmental awareness across two zones.
