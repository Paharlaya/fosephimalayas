Home/Get involved

Every hand on the mountain matters.

Protecting the Eastern Himalaya is work we do together — with members who stand behind us, volunteers who show up, and supporters who keep our camps and plantations going.

Why it matters

Conservation that's led by community.

The hills are protected best by the people who live in them. When you join FOSEP, you strengthen a network of communities, schools, and Gram Panchayats working to keep the Himalaya healthy for the next generation. There are three ways to step in.

Membership

Become a member

Stand with FOSEP as a member and help sustain environmental protection across the Darjeeling Hills and Siliguri. Members give our work roots — a community that carries our mission forward, season after season.

Volunteer

Lend your hands

Join us in the field — at health camps, tree plantations, nature camps, and cleanup drives across the hills. Whatever your skills, there's a place for you on the ground.
