Latest updates

Out in the field and the community.

From high-altitude observances at Sandakphu to health camps at Chowrasta, here's what FOSEP has been up to.

World Environment Day · 2022

“Let's protect & conserve biodiversity”

FOSEP marked World Environment Day 2022 at Sandakphu, renewing its pledge to the fragile high-altitude ecosystem of the Singalila ridge.

Health camp · 14 Dec 2022

HIV screening & health camp at Chowrasta

A free HIV screening and health camp (FOSEP-TI) in the heart of Darjeeling, spreading testing and awareness that support begins with understanding.

World AIDS Day · 2022

Standing with people living with HIV

World AIDS Day awareness activities — carrying the red-ribbon message of support and dignity across the community.

Health camp · 2022

Free medical health check-up camp

Free check-ups brought basic healthcare closer to communities that live far from it.

World Water Day · 2022

Guarding the springs of the hills

World Water Day awareness on protecting the springs and sources that the Darjeeling hills depend on.

World Heritage Day · 2022

Celebrating our natural heritage

Marking World Heritage Day with a spotlight on the natural and cultural heritage of the Eastern Himalaya.

Disaster response

Landslide relief, led by the community

Clearance and relief after a landslide — part of FOSEP's ongoing disaster-preparedness work in vulnerable areas.
