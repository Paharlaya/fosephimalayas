The mountain's standing watch.

For decades, the Federation of Societies for Environmental Protection has worked at the meeting point of people and the Himalaya — where conservation, health, and livelihood are one and the same task.

Who we are

A federation rooted in the hills.

FOSEP — the Federation of Societies for Environmental Protection — is a registered non-governmental organisation working across the Darjeeling Hills and the Siliguri region of the Eastern Himalaya. We are registered under the West Bengal Society Registration Act, 1961.

Our work spans one of the most biodiverse and ecologically fragile landscapes on Earth: the Kanchenjunga landscape, home to globally threatened endemic plants, animals, insects, and birds, and to communities whose well-being is inseparable from the health of the mountain.

A mountain environment where there would be no conflict between human ambition and the natural processes of evolution of life and eco-systems.

That vision guides everything we do — from biodiversity conservation to health camps, from afforestation to disaster preparedness.

Our mandate

The Ministry of Environment & Forests has designated FOSEP as a Regional Resource Agency (RRA) for National Environment Awareness Campaign (NEAC) activities across the Darjeeling Hills and Siliguri — a role we carry with care and accountability.

How we work

  • Alongside tea-garden communities and Gram Panchayats, not above them.
  • With location-specific models that fit the terrain and the people.
  • In partnership with government, schools, and frontline forces like the SSB.

Built on values

What we stand for.

Stewardship

The Himalaya is not a resource to spend but a living system to keep. We measure success in habitats protected and species that endure.

Equity

Conservation only lasts when it is just. We work for gender equity, social and economic justice, and the fair sharing of natural resources.

Community

Lasting change is local. Our models are designed with the communities who live closest to the land and depend on it most.
